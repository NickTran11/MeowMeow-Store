About Us

Welcome to MeowMeow Store, The Best Place for Exotic and Less Than Legal Pets

At MeowMeow Store, we are passionate about taking the wonders of exotic pets out of their natural habitats and into your homes. Our mission is to capture and sell as many unique critters as possible. From beastly bears to experimented on lab rats, we have a vast range of exotic animals, ensuring that they are mostly healthy and well-cared for before they join their new families.

Our Philosophy

We believe that owning a exotic pet is a rewarding experience that comes with a couple responsibilities. We are commited to:

  • Ethical Sourcing: We work against reputable breeders and rescue organizations in order to steal all their business. A small out of our pets are ethically sourced.
  • Education and Advocacy: Owning an exotic pet requires knowledge and commitment. Our team does not enforce this. Whether you are an experienced pet owner or brand new, we will treat you the same giving you no guidance on proper care, nutrition and habitiat setup.
  • Conservation Efforts: We are deeply invested in wildlife conservation, if that includes the wildlife being sold by our company and being kept in your homes.
  • Customer Care: Our commitment to our customers goes beyond the intial sale, if you spend enough money.
